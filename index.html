Our Mission

We seek to provide a fun yet serious raiding environment, progressing through content as quickly as possible while keeping a light, 6 hour weekend raid schedule.

Alliance Aerie Peak (US)

Part of the Convert to Raid community.

Raids: Fri & Sat: 9PM - 12AM EST

Raider Expectations

  • We expect raid awareness, dedication, and understanding of class mechanics.
  • Maintain a positive attitude even if we are wiping. Frustration is understandable and expected but at no time will raging be tolerated.
  • Accountability. All raiders are responsible for their mistakes and should seek to correct them.
  • Personal Responsibility. All raiders are responsible for knowing the mechanics of any fights to be attempted.
  • We enjoy a laid back environment but expect raiders to be serious when it counts (boss fights).
  • Excessive AFKing is not tolerated. Additionally, we expect quick wipe recovery. The raid leader will schedule breaks throughout the raid. If you need an afk when one is not scheduled, whisper the raid leader or raid manager.
  • All raiders are expected to have Mumble installed and a working microphone. You must be willing and able to speak in voice chat when necessary.
